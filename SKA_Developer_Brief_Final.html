About the Company

Sarah Khan Associates (SKA) is a behavioural intelligence firm based in Karachi, incorporated February 2026. We build the research and tools Pakistan's corporate sector needs to measure and close the gap between what organisations invest in developing their people and what actually changes in how those people behave at work.

SKA is Anchor Organisation Lead for Harvard EASEL Lab's PSS-SEL Toolbox Phase II and a selected participant in the Standard Chartered WomenInTech Accelerator Cohort 8. Our flagship product — the Thrive by Design Diagnostic — is Pakistan's first behavioural screening instrument for adult professionals. The front-end is fully built and live. We need a backend developer to make it work.

What the Product Does

The Thrive by Design Diagnostic is a 15-minute, 12-item assessment for corporate teams. A user enters an access code, completes the diagnostic, and submits their responses. The founder then receives those responses, generates a Behavioural Gap Report, and delivers it to the client.

The front-end — the website, the diagnostic interface, the access-code gate, the form — is already built and live. What does not exist yet is the backend: nothing currently happens when a user hits submit. Your job is to build that missing piece.
